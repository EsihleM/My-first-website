Caramel cake is a moist, sweet sponge cake with layers of sponge smothered in a deep caramel frosting. Cake tends to be made up of butter, sugar, eggs, and flour, while the frosting is created through the caramelization of sugar and mixing it with butter and cream to achieve a deep, buttery flavor. Some are topped with a drizzle or filling of caramel for extra indulgence. Used in Southern desserts, the caramel cake is a favorite dessert for special occasions..
